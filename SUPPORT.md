# Support

## Before reporting an issue

Please check that:

- you are using the latest available version of the extension;
- the conversation is opened on `https://chatgpt.com/`;
- you are logged in to ChatGPT in the same Firefox profile;
- the conversation is fully accessible in the browser;
- the issue still occurs after refreshing the ChatGPT tab.

## Useful information to provide

When reporting a bug, include:

- Firefox version;
- extension version;
- export mode used: readable conversation or complete archive;
- export format used: JSON or HTML;
- whether the integrated button or the Firefox toolbar popup was used;
- a short description of the expected result;
- a short description of the actual result.

Do not share private conversations publicly unless you have reviewed and redacted them.

## Diagnostic exports

If an export problem is difficult to reproduce, a diagnostic export may help identify whether the issue comes from the data returned by ChatGPT or from the HTML/JSON rendering layer.

Review diagnostic files before sharing them. They may contain conversation metadata.

## Privacy reminder

ChatGPT Conversation Export does not use a third-party server. Exports are generated locally by the browser.
